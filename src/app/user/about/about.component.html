About App

The canteen app is a comprehensive solution designed to enhance the overall dining experience by streamlining the process of accessing canteen services. It offers a user-friendly interface that enables users to view the time of breakfast, lunch, and snacks, along with the menu and pricing for each food item.

In addition to providing menu information, the app allows users to send feedback on the food they have purchased, which can be used to improve the quality of the food and the overall dining experience for all users.
